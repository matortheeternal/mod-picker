You can earn a month of free premium access for every 30 reputation earned by making contributions to the site. This free access will only start when you choose to activate it from this page. Below is an outline of how much reputation different contributions earn:

  • Submitting a mod: 1 reputation
  • Submitting a review or note: 2 reputation
  • Submitting a correction: 1 reputation
  • Creating a new tag: 0.2 reputation
  • Adding a tag to a mod or mod list: 0.1 reputation

You can read more about how reputation works on the reputation help page.
